DISTRIBUTION OF ITEM PRICE¶

ITEM PRICE VS ITEM STORE RETURNS¶

The higher the price of an item, the higher its return.

STORE SIZE ITEM PRICE VS ITEM RETURNS¶

Small and Medium sized stores were not stocked with items with prices less than N220. Most of the high priced items are in the small sized store. Studying the Medium sized store however, many items are within the price 250 and 500 and their returns are high compare to the other store sizes. Interestingly, The High store sizes were stocked with items with the least price. No wonder the returns were not very high there as seen in a previous visualization
